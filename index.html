advisor: Dr. Mohammad Shifat-E-Rabbi domain: Computer Vision · Medical AI status: Completed

Deep Convolution Neural Networks with Knowledge Distillation for Diabetic Retinopathy Grading

This research develops a computationally efficient deep learning framework for automated diabetic retinopathy severity classification — tackling one of the leading causes of preventable blindness worldwide. A high-capacity DenseNet121 teacher model was trained to learn discriminative retinal features, then its knowledge was transferred to a lightweight SmallCNN student model via logit-based knowledge distillation. The result: competitive diagnostic accuracy with dramatically reduced model size and computational cost, enabling AI-powered DR screening on edge devices and in resource-constrained clinical environments.
